Player Representative

EFMA not only strives to be a world class player management agency, we help provide professional representation for football players on legal contract, endorsement and many more as we protect players interest around the clock at the same time laying the most suitable and desirable grounds and outcome for our players growth and development.

Managing of Players

Our utmost priority lies in managing our clients by securing the best possible condition for all our players as this is key in boosting our players trust which assist them in exploring and demonstrating their level-best input. The world is theirs for the taking and we, EFMA, are the wings that make our players soar through the limitless opportunities in the realm of possibilities the world has to offer.

To meet up with top-best factors in the business, EFMA passionately runs its very own football academy from U11 to U18. We believe in grassroots football investment and development as this gives a crystal clear future for our players; helping the rising stars radiate in football throughout the world.

Football Consultancy

We provide football consultancy training to football clubs, players, coaches and football personnel to help them keep up with the evolution and growth of the beautiful game at every level both international and locally.
